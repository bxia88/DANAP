Perforated Ear Drums

Question:

Burst / Perforated Ear Drums. Do They Heal?

Answer:

Yes. Burst eardrums usually heal within a few weeks although in severe cases surgery could be required to repair a badly damaged eardrum. The diver should have the ear checked by a doctor before returning to diving.
